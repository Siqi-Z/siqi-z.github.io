Current Project

  • Stochastic First-order Methods for Nonsmooth Nonconvex Optimization

    • Check the convergence performances of some basic first-order methods like stochastic gradient/mirror descent in nonsmooth nonconvex optimization.

    • Relax common assumptions to see whether these algorithms can still converge.

    • Apply existing convergence guarantees to several important nonconvex optimization problems and design better first-order methods.
