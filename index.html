Introduction

Hi, this is Siqi Zhang, I am a 5-th year PhD student in the ISE Department of UIUC, also I am affiliated with CSL, supervised by Prof. Niao He.

Since August 2021, I have moved to the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich as a visiting Ph.D. student.

Research Intersts

Broadly speaking, I am interested in optimization and its applications in machine learning. Recently, I am focusing on the following topics:

  • Nonconvex optimization

  • Min-max optimization

  • Lower bound analysis in optimization

  • Intersection of opitmization with sampling, control and reinforcement learning
