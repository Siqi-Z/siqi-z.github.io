Introduction

Hi, my name is Siqi Zhang. Since the fall of 2017, I am a PhD student in the ISE Department of UIUC, working with Prof. Niao He.

I am mainly interested in optimization theory, algorithms and its applications in machine learning.

I received my B.S. majoring in mathematics from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech, formerly SUSTC), Shenzhen, China, in 2017.
